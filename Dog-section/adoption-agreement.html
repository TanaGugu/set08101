Beastly Bonds Adoption Contract

This Adoption Contract ("Contract") is made and entered into by and between Beastly Bonds ("Rescue") and the adopter ("Adopter").

1. Adoption Details

The Adopter agrees to provide a loving, safe, and permanent home for the pet.

2. Care Requirements

Adopter agrees to provide proper food, water, shelter, veterinary care, and humane treatment at all times.

3. No Transfer

Adopter agrees not to sell, give away, or otherwise transfer ownership of the pet.

4. Return Policy

If Adopter can no longer care for the pet, Adopter must return the pet to Beastly Bonds.

5. Compliance with Laws

Adopter agrees to comply with all local laws regarding pet ownership.
