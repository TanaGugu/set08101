About Buddy

Buddy is a lovable Labrador Retriever with a heart of gold. He's full of energy and loves playing fetch, going for long walks, and swimming. Buddy is extremely friendly and gets along well with children, other dogs, and even cats!

Buddy's Story

Buddy came to us when his previous family moved overseas and couldn't take him with them. He's been with our shelter for 3 months and has been a favorite among our volunteers. Buddy knows basic commands like sit, stay, and come, and he's house-trained. He would thrive in an active family that can give him plenty of exercise and attention.
