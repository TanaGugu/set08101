Got room for One More?

Maybe it’s the corner by the window, the empty chair at the table, or the silence when you come home. Fill that space with a grateful wag, a sleepy purr, or a cheerful chirp.

Meet Bongo

Bongo was found curled inside a rain-soaked cardboard box outside a city café. We brought him home, dried his fur, and set a throw pillow on the windowsill. Ten minutes later he was sprawled like royalty, surveying pigeons with the calm of a retired barista. Now he’s searching for a forever sofa—preferably one with morning sun and a human who doesn’t mind sharing their cappuccino foam.
