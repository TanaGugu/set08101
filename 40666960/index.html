Meet Bongo

Bongo sat alone on a rocky outcrop, ears pinned back, stunned by how different everyone else seemed. He felt like that stray nut nobody wanted. Until one day, he cracked open a special banana and discovered an entire troupe waiting with cheers—just for him. His jaws unhinged in joyful surprise, Bongo realized that sometimes, the scariest leap brings you exactly where you belong. Join us and find your perfect community—you might be surprised how quickly you’ll fit right in.
